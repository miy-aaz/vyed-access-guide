Overview

This guidance is for providers using VYED and outlines the key steps in requesting access to the service.

If you’re an approver, read the approver guide.

You’ll need a DfE Sign-in account to access VYED. You can create a DfE Sign-in account if you do not have one.

Check if you already have access to VYED

  1. Sign in to your DfE Sign-in account
  2. Select ‘Services’ from the navigation bar.
  3. Look for ‘View your education data (VYED)’ in the list of services.

If VYED is not listed, follow the steps below to request access.

Request access to VYED for the first time

  1. Sign in to your DfE Sign-in account
  2. Select ‘Services’ from the navigation bar.
  3. Under ‘Related actions’, select ‘Add services to my account’.
  4. Select your organisation if prompted and click ‘Continue’.
  5. Select ‘View your education data (VYED)’ and any required sub-services.
  6. Review your answers and click ‘Add service’.
{{ govukInsetText({ text: "If you’re registered with multiple organisations, repeat these steps for each one." }) }}

For more information on services, read the services guidance on DfE Sign-in.

Request access to a new organisation

  1. Sign in to your DfE Sign-in account
  2. Select 'Organisations' from the navigation bar.
  3. Under ‘Related actions’, select ‘Request access to an organisation’.
  4. Search for your organisation using the search bar.
  5. Select your organisation.
  6. Review the organisation details, provide a reason for your request and click ‘Confirm’.

For more information on organisations, read the organisations guidance on DfE Sign-in.

After you request access to an organisation or VYED

We’ll send an email to your organisation’s approver once you request access.

You’ll get an email once they approve the request. This means you now have access.

You can check the status of your request in the ‘Requests’ section of your DfE Sign-in account.

How to contact your approver

  1. Sign in to your DfE Sign-in account
  2. Select 'Organisations' from the navigation bar.
  3. Select your organisation from the list.
  4. Select ‘Show approvers’.

If an approver in your list has left your organisation, you can contact us via the Customer Help Portal.

If no approvers are listed, the DfE Sign-in team will verify your request. This can take up to 5 working days.
